## Obtaining Ether

Before undertaking any transactions, your Ethereum account needs Ether or test Ether.

- On public test networks, use the faucet or provider documentation for the network you are targeting.
- On local development networks, prefunded dev accounts are usually available by default.
- On mainnet, obtain Ether through your preferred exchange or custody workflow.

If you are just getting started with Web3j, a local dev network or Sepolia is usually the fastest path.
